# RQ008A Knowledge Synthesis

Status: accepted negative discovery boundary (knowledge-layer `decision.md` frozen 2026-06-24). Discovery-only; confirmation hold-out never opened.

RQ008 asked what reproducible temporal structures appear in joint human IPV mean and uncertainty as InterHub interactions form, roles emerge, risk changes, and negotiations resolve. Wave A was open discovery only: split discovery from confirmation before exploration, search broadly, apply mechanical and compositional controls, and freeze only a limited confirmation set. Confirmatory wording, external-outcome use, and manuscript claims from discovery-stage results were prohibited.

Completed run: `RQ008_1_temporal_ipv_discovery_20260622T234914+0800_3e3e776a`. Reader report: `RUN_ROOT/90_report/index.html`.

## What Was Found

The knowledge-layer result is **negative**. After the Phase-7 control gate, **0 of 24** fixed discovery candidates survived. The candidate set covered 18 motifs, 2 alignments, and 4 role/uncertainty effects (3 role/phase + 1 uncertainty/estimability). Every candidate failed at least one relevant control: motifs were mostly killed by reversed time; alignments by source balancing and uncertainty-only clustering; role/phase candidates by mechanical, pseudo-pair, duration, alignment, uncertainty, and estimability controls; the uncertainty candidate by pseudo-pair, duration-matched, and estimability-matched controls.

So RQ008A does **not** support a temporal IPV motif, a continuous feedback-adjustment story, reciprocity, lead-lag, risk-IPV coupling, hysteresis, or any IPV-mean temporal law. Descriptive atlas curves, alignment concentrations, role summaries, and motif labels remain exploratory audit material only.

## Why The Negative Is Credible

The discovery/confirmation split was established before exploration (22,937 discovery cases; 15,291 held-out confirmation never opened); the plan hash was pinned. The control phase was conservative and explicit (time-shuffle, reversed-time, pseudo-pair, duration-matched-null, random-alignment, source-balancing, uncertainty-only-clustering, estimability-matched). Passing descriptive screens alone was not enough, which is why the headline is 0/24 rather than "several promising motifs." Independent review ratified the freeze (`FROZEN_RQ008A`); the red team returned `PASS_WITH_FIXES` with zero blocking findings (nonblocking: source composition, clustering instability, Attack-10 false-negative risk); replication was partial (agreed on atlas curves, role formation, a negative-control check, and the split; change-point-bin divergence does not change 0/24 because change-point candidates were already rejected).

## What It Means For The Program

Lower confidence in broad, discovery-derived temporal-IPV narratives for this InterHub sigma=0.1 setting. Apparent temporal structure in the atlas is largely explained by time-reversal invariance, source composition, duration, and estimation uncertainty. The negative is a governance-grade boundary: temporal IPV hypotheses need controls that distinguish a directional temporal process from reverse-invariant shape and estimation artifacts. It prevents the manuscript and RQ009 from inheriting unconfirmed motif labels as social or behavioural laws. Consistent with RQ007, whose lifecycle is explicitly descriptive.

## Open Wave-B Question

Wave B is conditional and frozen. The primary null (`RQ008-EV-FROZEN-NULL-PRIMARY`) asks whether the held-out set also yields zero survivors after all controls and Holm correction; two falsification-only targets (MOTIF-008 joint-upward; early-minus-late uncertainty decline) may overturn it only if they pass the frozen high-bar controls on held-out data. Before any confirmation opening, the pre-Wave-B Attack-10 amendment is mandatory: add or pre-ratify a direction-sensitive companion motif statistic, or scope the null to directional temporal IPV structure that must exceed reverse-invariant shape. Without it, the reversed-time/SNR pairing is too direction-insensitive to rule out all reverse-invariant motif structure.

Decision: accept the negative discovery boundary; reject all positive temporal-law claims from RQ008A; keep Wave B frozen pending explicit authorization. See `decision.md`.
