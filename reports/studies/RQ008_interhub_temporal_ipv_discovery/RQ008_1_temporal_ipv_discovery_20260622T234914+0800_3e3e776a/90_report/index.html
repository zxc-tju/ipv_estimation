Executive Summary

Negative result: Phase-7 controls eliminated all 24 fixed discovery candidates: 18 motifs, 2 alignments, 3 role/phase candidates, and 1 uncertainty/estimability candidate. The correct headline is therefore 0/24 survive.

The discovery phase did find descriptive atlas shapes, alignment concentrations, role/phase summaries, and motif labels. Confirmation is not run here. The held-out `confirmation_PROTECTED` data remain closed until a separate Wave-B authorization, fixed-code review, and the pre-Wave-B amendments in `pre_wave_b_amendments.md`.

  • Geometry imbalance: `MP` contributes 20,719 cases, 90.3% of discovery.

Motifs And Null Attempts

Phase 6 attempted six motif methods and retained failures, fallbacks, and qualified rows instead of hiding them. The catalogue has 18 exploratory motif rows; 16/18 passed within-source screens before the stricter Phase-7 control gate eliminated all 18.

  • PCA/fPCA top variance explained: PC1 = 20.7% (about 21%), PC2 = 10.9%, PC3 = 6.4%. No dominant single shape controls the trajectory space.
  • Methods attempted: shape k-means, joint sequence k-means, change-point bins, observed-state HMM-style surrogate, DTW k-medoids, and PCA/fPCA axes.
  • The HMM result is explicitly an observed-state Markov/HMM-style surrogate because full latent HMM fitting was unavailable without `hmmlearn`/`scipy`.
  • Red-team reseeding found only moderate label stability: selected shape k=6 mean ARI 0.492; joint sequence k=3 mean ARI 0.691.
  • Negative attempts retained in `tried.md`: missing dependency fallbacks, source/path artifact caveats, change-point sensitivity, DTW subsample limits, and candidate rows failing within-source screens.

The required pre-Wave-B amendment is recorded in `02_process/09_hypothesis_freeze/pre_wave_b_amendments.md`.
