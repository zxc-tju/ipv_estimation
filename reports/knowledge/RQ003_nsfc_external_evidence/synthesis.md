## What The Reports Currently Suggest

NSFC data can support external-validation design, hypothesis mining, and
competition-outcome anchoring. It should not yet be used as a frozen formal IPV
verifier-validation claim without additional mapping and normative-envelope
checks.

The RQ003_6 confirmatory run tested this directly on the approved top-five
cohort (N=53) and returned a power-limited boundary/null: no robust incremental
predictive utility for directional IPV over the prespecified kinematic+safety
baseline, with the favorable direction matched or exceeded by negative controls
(not IPV-specific) and not generalizing across scenarios (LOSO). This is frozen
in `decision.md` as Tier B diagnostic/boundary evidence and supports the
manuscript only as a guardrail and ready validation protocol, not as a positive
external-validation result.
