# Training Vs Formal Separation

Training material is fictional practice material. It may be discussed during training to calibrate use of the codebook.

Formal validation material is labeled for real by independent human annotators. Training answers must not be copied, adapted, or used as evidence for any formal item. Formal labels must be based only on the visible behavior in the assigned item.

The codebook author did not fill formal labels. This package contains no real labels.
